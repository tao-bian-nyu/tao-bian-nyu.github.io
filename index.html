Hi there, I'm Tao Bian!

Currently, I am an a quantitative analyst at WorldQuant. I am also an Associate Editor of IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems. I received the B.Eng. degree in automation from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China, in 2012, and the M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical engineering from New York University, NY, in 2014 and 2017, respectively.

I have a broad research interests in reinforcement learning, systems and control theory, stochastic systems, and their applications to computational & systems neuroscience, power system analysis, and quantitative & systematic trading. Please come here to find more about my research.
